Latest works

Automated Inspection using Deep Learning (AIDL) MRO Application

(AIDL) is a cloud-based software platform I helped to develop as Principal Investigator at Analatom, Inc., designed to automate visual and subsurface inspections across defense and aerospace applications. AIDL leverages deep learning and hyperspectral imaging to identify defects in complex materials—such as composites used in aircraft structures—with greater speed and accuracy than traditional methods. The platform has been deployed for inspections on C-130 and F-15 aircraft components and is currently being transitioned into secure DoD cloud environments for broader MRO use.
